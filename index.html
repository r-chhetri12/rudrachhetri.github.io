Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

SQL Python Chi-square Test Power BI

Analyzed ~7K telecom customers to understand why customers are leaving, which segments are most at risk, and how churn impacts revenue.

  • Overall churn rate: 27% (~1 in 4 customers), making churn a critical business issue.
  • Customers with 0–1 month tenure have the highest churn rate at 62%, indicating serious onboarding and early-experience problems.
  • Senior citizens (60+) churn at 35% vs 22% for customers under 30.
  • Lost revenue due to churn is $8M, about 26% of total revenue (~$31M).
  • Fiber Optic and month-to-month contracts (46% churn) are the riskiest segments, while longer contracts (1-year at 11%, 2-year at 3%) are much more stable.
  • Used chi-square tests to check association between churn and variables like Internet Type, Tenure Bucket, Contract, Streaming services, and Payment Method.

HR / Employee Attrition Analysis

SQL Python Power BI

Explored employee data to understand attrition patterns, identify high-risk groups, and highlight departments and roles with the most exits.

  • Overall attrition rate around 50%+, meaning over half of employees have left the company.
  • Highest attrition in the first year, then gradually dropping by year 5, indicating onboarding and early engagement issues.
  • Older employees (46+) and even high performers show higher attrition, signalling problems with career progression and retention strategy.
  • The Production department and roles like Laborer, Technician, Engineer, and Foreman show the highest attrition, pointing to workload and frontline pressure.
  • Built a Power BI dashboard to track attrition by tenure, age band, department, work-life balance, gender, and job role.

Sales Performance Dashboard

Power Query SQL DAX Power BI

Built an end-to-end sales performance dashboard with dynamic measures to analyze sales, profit, cost, shipments and boxes across regions, categories, products, and months.

  • Total sales: $34M, total profit: $21M (profit margin 60.29%), total cost: $14M, total shipments: 6k, total boxes: 2M.
  • New Zealand is the top region with $6M in sales and $4M in profit.
  • Bars is the highest-performing category with $17M in sales and $10M in profit.
  • Top salesperson Marney O’Breen generated $17M sales and $10M profit.
  • Peanut Butter Cubes has the highest product-level profitability with a 66.71% profit margin.
  • December shows the highest sales, profit, and shipments; October has the highest boxes, while January has the highest costs.
  • Used a DAX-based Measure Selector to dynamically switch a monthly trend chart between Sales, Boxes, Shipments, Profit, and Total Cost.
